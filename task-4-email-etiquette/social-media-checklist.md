### Social Media Etiquette Checklist for Students 


DO'S: 

      * Do update your privacy settings regularly to protect your personal data from unauthorized access. 

      * Do use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase your academic projects and technical certifications. 

      * Do verify the source and accuracy of information before sharing it with your network. 
      
      * Do maintain a respectful and professional tone when engaging in public discussions or forums. 
      
      * Do give proper credit to original creators when sharing copyrighted content or code. 


DON'TS: 

        * Don't post sensitive personal information, such as your live location or student ID card.  
        
        * Don't share screenshots of private university communications without explicit permission. 
        
        * Don't use informal language or slang when communicating in professional or academic groups. 
        
        * Don't engage in cyberbullying, harassment, or heated arguments in comment sections. 
        
        * Don't post content that could negatively impact your future professional reputation or "digital footprint."
